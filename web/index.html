Math-O-Matic

Help your child improve their mental arithmetic. Math-O-Matic is designed to enable parents to actively assist their children practice and improve their mental arithmetic.

  • Configure a collection of exercises to individually suite your child's unique needs.
  • Monitor your child's progress. Discover where your child has difficulties.
  • Adjust the program to support you child further.

Play online

Features

  • Define a program as a set of individual exercises. The duration of each exercise and therefore the duration of the whole exercise program can be determined precisely. It is important that children are not burdened for too long with exercises which are too difficult. Regular practice, even when not very long, is more effective than irregular practice.
  • Exercises can be stipulated as optional or mandatory for the practice program.
  • Exercises which you have defined can be easily switched on or off in the practice program, so you can quickly adjust which exercises are practiced.
  • Construct individual exercises from a selection of exercise templates:

    Practice Multiplication Tables

    Practice multiplication tables by repetition. Eg. 2x4=, 3x4=, 4x4=...

    Test Multiplication Tables

    Test multiplication tables by randomly selecting multiplications from a range of values. Eg. 8x7, 3x2, 4x5...

    Total Sum

    Practice mental tallying. A running total must be remembered whilst adding additional values to the total. Eg. 40+4+5+6, where the numbers displayed are 40, +4, +5 and +6= respectively.

    Upperbounded Sum

    Given some maximum value, say 100, we choose two random numbers who's sum is < 100. Eg. 45+32, 10+39.
  • The configuration is protected with a PIN code.
  • View the results of each exercise at any time. Discover patterns from mistakes and discover the speed of mental calculation.
  • Visualization of the result history of each exercise. Be able to tell if your exercise program is actually helping your child.
